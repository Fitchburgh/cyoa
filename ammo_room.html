As I entered the ammo room...

My gut wrenched in pain, still reeling from the experience in the gunroom. I could hear something below me. Which turned the adrenaline on. With my heightened senses, I peered out the tiny hole they called a window on these old ships and swore I saw a fin swimming down below the hull. Above me another blasted hole which opened into a dark room, however my headlight happened upon a glimmer which looked like gold. Could this be the Coil I sought?!
